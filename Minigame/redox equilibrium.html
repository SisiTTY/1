🧪 REDOX DEFENDER
Form 5 Chemistry | Oxidation & Reduction

🎮 HOW TO PLAY (Easy & Fun!)

⭐ Your Goal: Protect the iron bar from rusting by matching the correct beam to each falling droplet!

🔫 HOW TO SHOOT: Simply CLICK or TAP on any droplet. The game will use your selected beam.

⚡ CHOOSE YOUR BEAM: Click the colored buttons below to switch beams before shooting.

✅ CORRECT MATCH = +POINTS & COMBO
❌ WRONG MATCH = -POINTS & COMBO BREAK
💧 If droplet hits bottom = RUST DAMAGE (-15 points)

📚 REDOX RULES (Quick Reference):
🔴 OXIDATION = loss of electrons → shoot with 🔴 OXIDATION beam
🔵 REDUCTION = gain of electrons → shoot with 🔵 REDUCTION beam
🟡 VOLTAIC CELL = spontaneous reaction → shoot with 🟡 VOLTAIC beam
🟢 ELECTROLYTIC CELL = needs electricity → shoot with 🟢 ELECTROLYTIC beam
🟤 RUSTING = iron oxidation → shoot with 🔴 OXIDATION beam
🟣 METAL EXTRACTION = reduction of ore → shoot with 🔵 REDUCTION beam
